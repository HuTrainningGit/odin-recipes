Indian Chicken Curry (Murgh Kari)

Description

Enjoy the lavishly delicious taste of chicken curry at home with this easy-to-follow recipe. Bold spices like ginger, cumin, and curry powder add rich layers of flavor to succulent chicken coated in a creamy tomato-based sauce. Fill your kitchen with the delightful fragrance of a creamy, spicy, and filling curry that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • Garam masala
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Tomato
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Freshly minced garlic and ginger
  • Chicken meat (only one is sufficient):
    • Skinless, boneless chicken breasts
    • Tenderloin
    • Thigh

Steps

  1. Partially cooking your seasoned chicken breasts in a large pan, removing them after they're browned.
  2. setting the chicken breasts aside, cook garlic, onions, and ginger in the same pan to create the flavor base.
  3. Once the onions are translucent, add in spices, tomatoes, yogurt, and chicken to cook thoroughly.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes for a wow-worthy chicken curry.
  5. Finish with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon.
